Data Simulating and Modeling Prep

The functions used for data simulation produce variables that are not model ready. In this document, we go over the functions in detail and describe the process we took to arrive at the humn-vs-bot modeling data. There is much room for improvemnet and modifications to allow more unique data.

There are four functions we uitilze for the human-vs-bot data:

  • human_startTimes
  • human_timesDistributionDF
  • bot_startTimes
  • bot_timesDistributionDF
The *_startTimes for bots and humans are anagolous as are the *_timesdistributionDF functions, except the second pair. have differnt response behavior time distributions

_startTimes Funcitons

These functions are anagoulous, they create for each human and bot a unique pair of ids: case and session depending on a start time.

_timesdistributionDF

Time between responses follow a normal distribution for the human functions while the time between responses follow an exponential distribution for bots.
